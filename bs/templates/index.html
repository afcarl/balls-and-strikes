Balls & Strikes

A Broadsheet of Baseball Tools and Reading

Databases

Databases and applications which aggregate stats and information from all eras of the game.

  • Retrosheet — Download highly detailed, easily parseable play-by-play logs of nearly every game ever played.

    See also...

    • Chadwick — Extend and enhance Retrosheet play-by-play data

    • retrosheet-queries — A cookbook of Chadwick-parsed Retrosheet database queries for PostgreSQL

    • retrosheet-pitch-sequences — A Python app which extracts all ball-strike counts from a Retrosheet pitch sequence

  • Chadwick Registry — A Rosetta stone which maps players to canonical IDs across many systems: Retrosheet, Fangraphs, Baseball Reference, Baseball Prospectus, NPB, and many more.
  • Player ID Map — Similar to the Chadwick Registry, this spreadsheet connects players across many systems. Unlike the Registry, this includes ESPN, CBS, Yahoo, and NFBC IDs.
  • Lahman Database — Sean Lahman's database offers “complete batting and pitching statistics from 1871 to 2013, plus fielding statistics, standings, team stats, managerial records, post-season data, and more.”
  • Pitch F/X database — Updated-daily database of Pitch F/X data from MLB.com
  • Disabled List Data — Disabled list information collected from MLB's records
